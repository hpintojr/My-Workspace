# 8.5 × 8.5 Layout Guide

## Canonical visual master
Finished reading page: **8.5 × 8.5 inches**.

## Separate output copies
- Digital PDF: clean reading pages only; no printer guides or print-only content.
- Lulu paperback: provider-specific production interior.
- Lulu hardcover: provider-specific production interior.
- Locales: duplicate approved layout, then reflow text and adjust only what is needed.

## Cover rule
Do not finalize a spine, cover width, or wrap-cover PDF until final page count and the current Lulu template for that exact binding have been recorded.
