# Polly Pump — Character Lock

**Status:** LOCKED (visual canon) · Source: approved reference `8745.png` (also seen on Book 1 Page 7 / `0449.png`)
**Reference:** `8745.png` (the three device helpers together) · `0449.png` (Polly held by Nurse Ivy) → `01-characters/_reference-images/`
**Precedence rule:** approved reference image wins over text.

> Polly Pump is the friendly **infusion-pump** helper — she gives the medicine a steady push "at just the right speed." One of three device helpers; **only one helper is used at a time** in a given scene.

## Locked appearance (from approved art)
- **Purple, smartphone/tablet-shaped body** — tall rectangle with rounded corners. *(Reads like a cute cartoon phone, not a flat box.)*
- **Light-blue "screen" face** with large bright eyes, **rosy cheeks**, and an open happy smile.
- **"Polly Pump"** lettered on the screen above the face.
- **Row of colorful round buttons** on the lower front, including a **pink heart button** in the middle (plus blue/yellow buttons).
- **Purple tubing** attached at the top.
- **Purple arms with rounded hands** (often one waving) and **purple legs with purple shoes.**
- Cheerful, friendly; looks proud to help. Often shown on/near the IV pole, tubing leading to Penny's IV.

## ⚠️ Correction vs. the written bible
The Author Bible text said Polly has a "mint-green bow." **The approved art shows NO bow on Polly.** Per the precedence rule, **lock = no bow** (only **Ellie** wears the mint bow). Flag for Michelle if she wants the bow added back.
