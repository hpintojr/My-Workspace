# Penny — Character Lock

**Status:** LOCKED (visual canon) · Source: Book 1 outline + Author Character & Series Bible v2.0
**Gold-standard references:** Book 1 cover · `3991.png` (Penny + Benny on the pink heart-shaped rug — approved likeness)
**Precedence rule:** If generated art ever conflicts with this text, the **approved reference image wins**. Match the reference, then fix anything this lock calls out.

> Penny is the heart of every book — a brave little girl who feels real, age-appropriate feelings and gets through hard medical days with her best friend Benny Bear. She must look like the **same 2–3 year old** on every page of every book. The #1 problem we are solving is drift: each AI page currently renders her slightly differently. This lock ends that.

---

## Core identity (LOCKED)

- **Age shown:** 2–3 years old — a toddler. Never older than preschool age.
- **Proportions:** Toddler build. Petite. Slightly shorter arms and legs than an older child. Big head-to-body ratio typical of a toddler.
- **Cheeks:** Round toddler cheeks, soft rosy blush.
- **Face:** Soft, youthful features. Small **button nose**. Sweet, curious smile.
- **Eyes:** Large brown eyes.
- **Hair:** Dark brown, **straight**. Two **low pigtails**. Soft bangs across the forehead.
- **Bows:** **Mint green** bows on the pigtails (one per pigtail).
- **Skin tone:** Warm light-to-medium (match approved references; keep consistent page to page).
- **Personality:** Curious, brave, sweet, gentle.
- **Constant companion:** Almost always holding, hugging, or seated beside **Benny Bear** when the scene allows.

## Wardrobe (LOCKED defaults)

- **Signature outfit:** Mint-green dress with a small white **heart pattern**. White sneakers with mint accents and white socks. *(This is her cover/title look and the default unless a page calls for something else.)*
- **Infusion-day comfort look:** Soft **mint pajamas or comfy play clothes** + a cozy mint blanket (used on the "getting ready / during infusion" pages). Still recognizably Penny — same hair, bows, face.
- Do not change the default dress design, bow color, pigtail placement, eye color, or facial proportions without a page-specific, author-approved reason.

## Expression range (for page briefs)

Happy/excited · curious/interested · a little nervous-but-safe · calm/relaxed · brave/proud · peaceful/sleepy. Penny **may look nervous**, but she is always clearly safe and supported — never scared, crying hard, or in distress.

## DO NOT DRIFT (hard rules)

- ❌ Do not age her up — no older child / "big kid" proportions or face.
- ❌ Do not change pigtails to a different style, move them, or drop the bows.
- ❌ Do not change bow color (mint), eye color (brown), or the button nose.
- ❌ Do not restyle the heart-pattern dress on her default/cover look.
- ❌ Do not make her skin tone darker or lighter than the approved reference.
- ✅ Keep her clearly younger than the patients a nurse would usually see — she reads as a sweet toddler.
