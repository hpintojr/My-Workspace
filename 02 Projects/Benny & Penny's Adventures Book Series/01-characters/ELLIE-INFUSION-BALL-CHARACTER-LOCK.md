# Ellie Infusion Ball — Character Lock

**Status:** LOCKED (visual canon) · Source: approved reference `8745.png`
**Reference:** `8745.png` (the three device helpers together) → `01-characters/_reference-images/`
**Precedence rule:** approved reference image wins over text.

> Ellie Infusion Ball is the gentle helper that **already holds the medicine inside** and **slowly squeezes it through the tubing** — no machine, no batteries, no beeping. She gets softer/smaller as she empties. One of three device helpers; only one helper is used at a time.

## Locked appearance (from approved art)
- **Cream/off-white, round ball body** (soft, balloon-like sphere).
- Large bright eyes, **rosy cheeks**, happy open smile.
- **"Ellie Infusion Ball"** lettered across the body.
- **Mint-green bow near the top** — ✅ Ellie is the **only** device helper who wears the mint bow.
- **Purple tubing** attached at the top.
- **Purple/cream arms with rounded hands** and **purple legs with purple shoes.**
- Sweet, gentle; looks happy and proud to help.

## Do not drift
- ❌ Don't drop her mint bow (it's her signature among the trio). ❌ Don't recolor the body away from cream/off-white.
- ❌ Don't make her look hard/mechanical — she's soft and round.
- ✅ Match `8745.png` exactly; cute and approachable, matching Polly Pump and Danny Dial-a-Flow in style.
