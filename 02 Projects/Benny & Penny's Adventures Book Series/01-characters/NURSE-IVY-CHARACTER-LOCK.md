# Nurse Ivy — Character Lock

**Status:** LOCKED (visual canon) · Source: Book 1 outline + Author Character & Series Bible v2.0
**Inspired by:** Michelle Marie Pinto, RN (the author).
**Gold-standard references:** Book 1 **Page 6** illustration (series standard for Ivy) · `0449.png` (**hair reference** — soft wavy, NOT too curly, in a low bun — the look Michelle likes)
**Precedence rule:** If generated art ever conflicts with this text, the **approved reference image wins**. Match Page 6 / the hair reference, then fix anything this lock calls out.

> Nurse Ivy is the warm, trusted home-infusion nurse who guides Penny (and the reader) calmly through every step. She must look like the **same person on every page of every book** — same face, same hair, same skin tone. Her likeness is personal to the author, so consistency here matters most.

---

## Hair (high-priority — see `0449.png`)

- **Dark brown.**
- **Soft wavy** hair worn in a **low bun**.
- **Smooth front pieces framing the face.**
- ❌ **NOT curly.** ❌ **NOT tight ringlets.** Keep waves soft and relaxed.
- Professional but gentle.

## Face & skin (LOCKED — do not alter)

- **Eyes:** brown.
- **Skin tone:** light to light-medium, **warm undertone**. ⚠️ **Keep the skin tone identical on every page — never make it darker** (or lighter). This is an explicit author rule.
- Warm smile, kind expression, soft natural makeup. Looks like a caring home-infusion nurse.
- **Facial consistency standard:** maintain the **same face shape, eye shape, eyebrow shape, and nose shape** across all books.

## Wardrobe & props

- **Teal scrubs** (top + matching pants).
- **Stethoscope** around the neck.
- **"NURSE IVY ❤" name badge** (white badge, mint/teal text, small pink heart).
- Often carries/uses a **nursing bag** and simple, child-friendly supplies on a clean table.

## Personality

Caring · calm · encouraging · reassuring. She kneels or sits to Penny's eye level, listens, explains each step simply, and praises Penny ("You're doing a great job, Penny!").

## DO NOT DRIFT (hard rules)

- ❌ Do not make her hair curly or in tight ringlets — soft waves in a low bun only.
- ❌ Do not darken (or lighten) her skin tone between pages or books.
- ❌ Do not change her face/eye/eyebrow/nose shapes.
- ❌ Do not change scrubs color (teal) or drop the stethoscope / "Nurse Ivy" badge.
- ❌ Never show needles in a scary way, a mask covering her whole face, or a stern expression — she is always calm, warm, reassuring.
- ✅ Match the approved Book 1 Page 6 likeness in every future book.
