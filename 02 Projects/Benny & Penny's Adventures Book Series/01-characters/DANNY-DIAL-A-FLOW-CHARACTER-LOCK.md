> Danny Dial-a-Flow is **not medicine** — he is the helper built into the tubing that **controls how fast the medicine drips**. One of three device helpers; only one helper is used at a time.

## Locked appearance (from approved art)
- **Purple, rounded body** (a soft vertical capsule / water-drop shape).
- Large bright eyes, **rosy cheeks**, happy open smile.
- **"Danny Dial-a-Flow"** lettered at the top of his body.
- **Round numbered dial on his tummy** — numbers (1–5 style) around a central knob, with small **"Dial up" / "Dial out"** labels beside it.
- **Purple tubing enters at the top and exits at the bottom** — he is **built into a section of the IV tubing**.
- **Purple arms with rounded hands** and **purple legs with purple shoes.**
- Friendly, playful; looks proud and excited to help.
