# Benny Bear — Character Lock

**Status:** LOCKED (visual canon) · Source: Book 1 outline + Author Character & Series Bible v2.0
**Gold-standard references:** Book 1 cover · `3991.png` (Penny + Benny on the pink heart-shaped rug)
**Precedence rule:** If generated art ever conflicts with this text, the **approved reference image wins**. Then fix anything this lock calls out.

> **Core identity:** Benny Bear is Penny's plush teddy-bear companion. He must always read as a **soft stuffed toy first, a bear second** — never a realistic bear, a real animal, or a teddy-bear "child." He appears identical in every book. He is Penny's constant source of comfort and bravery.

---

## Head & shape (MOST IMPORTANT — this is where AI drifts most)

- **Very large, round "marshmallow" head**, noticeably **wider than the body**.
- Perfectly rounded. ❌ No elongated skull, ❌ no oval head, ❌ no pointed forehead, ❌ no flat top.
- Head is **larger than the body**. Small plush body, round belly, soft stuffed-animal proportions.
- ⚠️ **AI common error:** makes Benny's head too tall or oval. Keep it round and wide.

## Face

- Face sits **low on the head**, leaving a **large forehead** above the eyes.
- **Small, centered, cream-colored muzzle**. Small smiling mouth, **tiny tongue often visible**. Small **dark brown nose**.
- Warm, friendly, comforting expression.
- ⚠️ **AI common error:** large muzzle or realistic bear snout. Keep the muzzle small and the snout flat/soft.

## Eyes

- **Very large, warm brown eyes**, widely spaced, glossy and toy-like, **round (not almond)**. Innocent, friendly.
- ⚠️ **AI common error:** eyes too small or too realistic.

## Ears

- **Small rounded ears**, low on the sides of the head. ❌ Not sticking up high, ❌ not oversized.
- ⚠️ **AI common error:** ears too large.

## Fur

- **Golden honey-brown** plush fur, **curly swirl plush texture**, soft stuffed-animal look, **uniform color**.

## NEVER ADD (these are NOT part of Benny)

- ❌ White forehead markings · ❌ white stripe on head · ❌ white tuft on top of head
- ❌ Any white muzzle markings beyond the approved cream muzzle
- ❌ Eyebrows made from fur markings

## Signature features (REQUIRED every appearance)

- **Mint-green bow tie.**
- **Mint-green heart patch on BOTH feet** (left and right paw pads) — required, do not omit.
- Friendly smile, round cuddly plush appearance.

## Identity & personality

- **Age:** ageless teddy-bear companion.
- **Personality:** loyal, gentle, encouraging, brave — Penny's best friend.
- Benny is a toy: he is posed, held, and present as comfort. Keep his expression warm and reassuring in every scene.

## DO NOT DRIFT (hard rules)

- ❌ Tall/oval head · ❌ large or realistic muzzle/snout · ❌ small or realistic eyes · ❌ oversized/high ears
- ❌ Any white head/forehead/muzzle markings or fur eyebrows
- ❌ Missing bow tie or missing foot-heart patches
- ✅ Round wide head bigger than body; small cream muzzle; big round glossy brown eyes; mint bow tie + mint heart on both feet.
