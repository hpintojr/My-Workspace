# Audio Export and QA

## Deliverables per language
- protected master audio
- website MP3
- narration script
- production notes/cue sheet
- upload manifest

## QA
- [ ] spoken text matches approved narration script
- [ ] medical terms and names are consistent
- [ ] no unapproved ad-libs
- [ ] opening/title/author correct
- [ ] no obvious clicks, clipping, accidental retakes, or excess silence
- [ ] consistent volume
- [ ] website MP3 plays correctly
- [ ] filename/version match manifest
