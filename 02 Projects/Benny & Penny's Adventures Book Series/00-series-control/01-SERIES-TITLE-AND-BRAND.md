# Series Title and Brand

## Official series title
**Benny & Penny’s Adventures**

## Official descriptor
**Medical Books for Brave Little Hearts**

## Reader promise
Each story explains a medical experience with warmth, accurate but child-safe wording, and the reassurance that children are safe, supported, and brave.

## Brand usage
Use the official series title on title pages, covers, website metadata, audio introductions, and release records.
