- **YouTube** — center of gravity: long-form fire tests, project tours, explainers for knowledge-seekers who research before they spec.
- **TikTok** — behind-the-scenes, raw/authentic top-of-funnel discovery.
- **X & Bluesky** — lightweight amplification.

## Posting-tool decision (open)

No first-party connector publishes to all 7 channels. Choice is **buy a scheduler vs. build a custom poster**.

- **Buy (recommended):** **Publer (~$12–16/mo)** best value for many accounts; **Buffer (~$30–36/mo)** simplest. Both publish to all platforms incl. TikTok/YouTube/Bluesky today.
- **Build:** Heavy lift — X API now ~$100+/mo; TikTok + LinkedIn require partner approval/app audits. Only worth it if cross-posting becomes a product you sell.
