Font Explorer

Each card shows your logo and both section headers in a candidate font. All are free Google Fonts that work with Next.js next/font/google. You can also mix — e.g., one font for the logo, a different one for the "Meet the…" headers.

My pick: Grand Hotel or Pacifico for the logo (warm + readable), Dancing Script for the "Meet the…" headers, and keep Nunito for body. Scripts like Pinyon/Sacramento look elegant but get hard to read small — better as accents than the logo.
