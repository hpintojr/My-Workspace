# SVG Editing Notes

Use `mcd-logo-horizontal-light.svg` on light backgrounds. Its wordmark is MCD Navy: `#071426`.

Use `mcd-logo-horizontal-dark.svg` on dark backgrounds. Its wordmark is white: `#FFFFFF`.

`MERCURY` and `CALL DESK` remain as editable SVG text. Open the source in Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Affinity Designer, or Inkscape to adjust text color, letter spacing, or the final approved font.

Keep the editable files as the master source. After the typeface is approved, make a second production release with text converted to paths for vendors that need it.
