# First 30 Days: Activity, Coaching, and Growth

## Daily operating rhythm

1. Open **My Follow-Ups Due Today** before beginning new outreach.
2. Complete all overdue follow-ups or notify your manager why a task needs to move.
3. Work your assigned new leads promptly.
4. Conduct planned outreach blocks.
5. Document every meaningful interaction before ending the day.
6. Leave every active deal with a clear next action and due date.

## Weekly review with your manager

Review:

- Number of new assigned leads
- Initial contact attempts completed
- Meaningful conversations
- Qualified opportunities
- Demos booked
- Demos held
- Proposals / checkout links sent
- Paid clients / wins
- Closed-lost reasons
- Stale leads and missed follow-ups
- Coaching themes and next-week focus

## First 30-day milestones

### Week 1

Complete onboarding, product learning, roleplay, CRM training, and certification.

### Week 2

Begin controlled live outreach; receive call and CRM feedback.

### Week 3

Build a predictable follow-up rhythm; complete qualified discovery calls and demos.

### Week 4

Review conversion metrics, strengths, gaps, and development plan with your manager.

## Professional standards

A successful Sales Partner is consistent, coachable, accurate in the CRM, respectful in communication, and focused on the customer’s actual business need. Activity matters, but quality and follow-through determine long-term residual success.
