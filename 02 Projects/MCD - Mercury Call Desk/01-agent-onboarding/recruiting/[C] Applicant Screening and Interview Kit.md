# Applicant Screening & Interview Kit

Use this to handle inbound applicants (Instagram DM @hpintojr or email) quickly and consistently — screen → interview → decide.

## 1. First reply / DM auto-response

When someone reaches out about the role:

> Thanks for reaching out about the AI Sales Partner role! 🙌 It's a remote, 1099, commission-only role with monthly residual income — your main job is connecting with businesses and booking demos (we support you on the demo). To see if it's a fit, can you answer a few quick questions?
> 1. What's your B2B / SaaS / cold-calling experience?
> 2. Are you comfortable with a 100% commission, residual-income model?
> 3. Are you set up to work remotely (computer, headset, reliable internet)?
> 4. About how many hours/week can you commit?
> 5. What's your time zone and best email?

## 3. Application scoring rubric (score 1–5 each; ~18+ / 30 = interview)

| Criterion | 1 (low) | 5 (high) |
|---|---|---|
| Relevant B2B/SaaS/cold-call experience | none | strong, recent, relevant |
| Communication (clear, professional in messages) | poor | excellent |
| Comfort with commission-only/residual | hesitant | fully on board |
| Self-starter / pipeline discipline | needs hand-holding | runs own book |
| Remote-ready & availability | minimal | well set up, real hours |
| Coachability / attitude | defensive | eager, asks good questions |

## 4. Structured interview guide (~20–30 min)

**Open (2 min):** thank them, set expectations — "This is a remote, commission-only partner role; I'll ask about your background, then explain how we work and the path to recurring income."

**Background & fit (8 min):**
- Walk me through your B2B/sales experience — what did you sell and to whom?
- Describe your typical outreach week (calls, follow-ups, pipeline).
- Tell me about a deal you closed start to finish.
- How do you handle a "we already have a phone system" objection?
- How do you stay organized — CRM, tasks, follow-up?

**Motivation & model (5 min):**
- How do you feel about a 100% commission, residual-income model with no base?
- What income are you working toward, and what's your runway to ramp?
- How many focused hours/week can you realistically commit?

**The role, explained (5 min):** AI call-automation platform; your #1 job is booking qualified demos; we support/lead demos at first; you grow into running them; monthly residuals + annual-contract upside; path to Reseller Partner. (Discuss the revenue-share structure here — confidential.)

**Their questions (5 min).**

**Close:** explain next steps (offer + agreement + NDA + W-9 + CRM access + certification before live leads).

## 5. Green flags / red flags

```txt
GREEN: real B2B closing experience · self-directed · organized · realistic about commission-only · coachable · professional communicator.
RED:   wants base salary/guarantees · can't articulate a sales process · over-promises / "I'll make you millions" · evasive about experience ·
       won't work in a CRM · conflicting contractual obligation that blocks this work · unprofessional messages · only wants the lead list.
```

## 6. Decision + reply templates

**Advance to onboarding:**
> Great talking with you, [Name]! I'd like to move you forward. Next steps: I'll send the Sales Partner Agreement, NDA, and W-9 to complete, then set up your CRM access and a quick certification before you get live leads. Sending those now — welcome aboard. 🚀

**Hold / nurture:**
> Thanks [Name]! You've got potential, but I'm finalizing the first cohort. I'll keep your info and reach out as we open more spots. Appreciate your interest!

**Decline (kind):**
> Thanks for your time, [Name]. For this round I'm looking for [specific gap, e.g., more B2B closing experience], so I won't move forward right now. I appreciate you reaching out and wish you the best.

## 7. Compliance notes
- Partners are independent contractors (1099) — avoid employee-style language ("hire," "salary," "manager-employee"); use "engage," "partner," "contractor."
- Don't promise specific earnings in screening or interviews.
- Keep pricing, commission math, and scripts confidential until after the NDA is signed.
