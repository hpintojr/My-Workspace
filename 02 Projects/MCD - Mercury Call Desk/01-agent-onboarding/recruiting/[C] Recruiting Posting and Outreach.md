## 1. Job Posting (approved — Indeed / LinkedIn / job boards)

**Job Title:** AI Sales Partner (B2B SaaS – Residual Commission)
**Location:** Remote
**Job Type:** Independent Contractor (1099)
**Compensation:** Commission Only + Recurring Revenue Share

### About the Opportunity
We are seeking experienced B2B sales professionals to help introduce businesses to our AI-powered telephony and call automation platform. Our solution answers inbound calls, routes customers, captures leads, and books appointments 24/7 — helping businesses improve customer service and reduce missed opportunities.

### What You'll Do
- Prospect and connect with small, medium-sized, and enterprise businesses
- Introduce business owners to our AI call automation platform
- Schedule product demonstrations (with company support)
- Build and maintain your own book of business
- Close new accounts and generate recurring revenue

### Compensation
This is a commission-only opportunity designed for sales professionals who want long-term recurring income.
- Ongoing monthly residual commissions for active client accounts
- Additional earnings available on annual contracts
- No cap on earnings
- Opportunity to advance into a Reseller Partner role as your client portfolio grows

Compensation details and revenue-sharing structure will be discussed during the interview process.

### Qualifications
- Experience in B2B sales, SaaS sales, cold calling, or business development
- Strong communication and relationship-building skills
- Ability to work independently and manage a sales pipeline
- Self-motivated and results-driven

### Why Join Us
- Sell a fast-growing AI business solution
- Build recurring monthly income
- Work remotely with a flexible schedule
- Unlimited earning potential

If you're looking to build a long-term residual income stream and have experience selling to businesses, we'd love to hear from you.

> Internal note: onboarding clarifies that, at first, partners **set the qualified demo** and the company joins/leads it (see `14_DEMO_PROCESS_AND_HANDOFF.md`); partners graduate to running demos solo.
