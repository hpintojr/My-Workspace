# Branded Sales Partner Launch Kit

## Purpose

This is the official company-branded onboarding package for authorized Mercury Call Desk Sales Partners. It consolidates the canonical agent-facing onboarding content into a polished 18-page launch booklet with the current Mercury Call Desk identity.

It is an operating and training package, not an employment agreement, commission contract, legal advice, or customer guarantee. The signed Sales Partner Agreement and approved company policies control if anything differs.

## Core operating rules preserved

- The first objective is a qualified, confirmed demo with the right decision-maker.
- Use the approved language, position a tailored demo, and never guarantee results, revenue, savings, compliance, staffing reduction, integrations, or timelines.
- A sample assistant is a representative demo. Never represent it as connected to the prospect's real systems unless that connection truly exists.
- All leads, activity notes, tasks, opportunities, and follow-up actions live inside the CRM.
- Opt-outs and Do Not Contact requests must be honored immediately.
- Complex integrations, regulated industries, custom pricing, unapproved features, and compliance questions must be escalated.
- Do not disclose the underlying platform vendor, internal costs, or confidential company materials.

## Agent-facing commission content

The package preserves the authorized-partner compensation framework:

- Flat 50% of gross profit.
- Gross profit = collected retail revenue - partner wholesale cost basis - Stripe processing fee.
- Trigger: cleared first payment.
- Monthly residuals: net-15 after collection.
- Annual commission: after annual funds clear.
- Refund / chargeback / cancellation window: 90 days for reversal or offset.
- Lead reassignment: no documented activity for 7 days.
- Company owns client records; residuals cease at termination.

The signed Sales Partner Agreement controls. This content remains confidential and is issued only after required confidentiality documents are completed.
