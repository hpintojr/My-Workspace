# Sales Partner Launch Kit Cover Revision

## Issue corrected

The original cover placed the Mercury Call Desk logo inside a visible rectangular raster block. On the dark cover, that created a pasted-on appearance and weakened the first impression of the agent package.

## Current cover standard

The updated cover integrates the Mercury Call Desk logo directly into the navy cover artwork.

- No hard-edged logo background box.
- No visible embedded image panel.
- Full wordmark remains readable against the cover.
- The `SALES PARTNER` / `LAUNCH KIT` hierarchy, purpose callout, and confidentiality footer remain intact.

## Delivery status

The corrected cover is now embedded in the current DOCX, PDF, and ZIP exports of the Sales Partner Launch Kit. The package has been rendered and visually reviewed after the replacement.
