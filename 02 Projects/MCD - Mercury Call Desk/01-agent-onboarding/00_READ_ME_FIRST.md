# Sales Partner Launch Kit

**Version 1.0 - Internal Confidential**  
**For:** Mercury Call Desk / Mercury Call Desk Platform Sales Partners  
**Issue Date:** June 2026

Welcome to the Sales Partner Launch Kit. This package is designed to give every new sales partner a clear, professional start: what we sell, who we serve, how to use the CRM, how to book demos, how compensation works, and what is required before a partner receives live leads.

## How to use this kit

1. Read `01_WELCOME_PACKET.md` first.
2. Complete the first-week requirements in `02_FIRST_7_DAYS.md`.
3. Learn the packages in `04_PRODUCT_CATALOG.md`.
4. Practice the outreach and discovery process in `06_OUTBOUND_CALL_SCRIPT.md` and `05_ICP_AND_DISCOVERY.md`.
5. Follow the CRM rules in `07_CRM_SOP.md` before working any lead.
6. Complete the certification scorecard in `09_CERTIFICATION_SCORECARD.md`.
7. Sign the acknowledgment in `12_NEW_HIRE_ACKNOWLEDGMENT.md`.

## Confidentiality

This kit contains internal operating materials, product positioning, pricing, commission structure, scripts, and workflow requirements. It is provided only to authorized sales partners after the company’s required confidentiality documents are completed. Do not reproduce, forward, publish, or share it outside the company without written approval.

## Important operating note

This is an onboarding and operations guide, not an employment agreement, commission contract, legal advice, or a customer guarantee. The signed sales-partner agreement, approved customer agreements, and approved company policies control if anything differs from this guide.
