# Sales Partner Launch Kit

**Version 2.0 — Internal Confidential**  
**For:** Charter Oaks Assets, Inc. d/b/a Mercury Call Desk Independent Sales Partners  
**Issue Date:** June 30, 2026

Welcome to the Mercury Call Desk Sales Partner Launch Kit. This package gives an approved Independent Sales Partner the operating tools, training, CRM expectations, lead rules, demo process, client-service standards, and commission framework needed to build business through MCD.

This is a business-in-a-box entry pathway. Partners independently control their schedules, location, prospecting methods, and non-competing business activity. MCD provides approved brand assets, CRM/portal access, customer contracts, implementation, billing, accounting, support intake, and Level 2 escalation. Completion of this kit does **not** guarantee income, results, lead volume, customer success, Advanced Partner authority, or future reseller status.

## Required access gate

**No paperwork, no access, no exceptions.** A Partner receives active MiniCRM/GHL access only after all of the following are complete and MCD countersigns:

1. Complete CRM profile with verified personal email, mobile number, mailing address, and required contact information.
2. W-9.
3. Signed Sales Partner Agreement.
4. Signed Confidentiality and Intellectual Property Agreement.
5. CRM training/check-in completion.

## Partner authority snapshot

- **Standard Sales Partner:** may prospect, use approved materials, quote published prices, and book qualified demos after CRM training.
- **Advanced Partner / Manager:** receives separate written approval to run demos, close authorized deals, and customize price above MCD's confidential floor.
- **Not granted by this kit:** a franchise, territory, ownership interest, reseller status, exclusive rights, authority to bind MCD, or authority to change customer contracts.
