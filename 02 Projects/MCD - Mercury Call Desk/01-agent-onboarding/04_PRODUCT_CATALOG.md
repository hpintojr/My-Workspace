# Product Catalog and Package Guide

## What we sell

Mercury Call Desk Platform provides AI-assisted telephony and call-desk automation designed to help businesses manage calls, capture leads, route customers, handle common questions, and support appointment workflows.

The exact solution is based on the customer’s business model, staffing, phone process, call volume, appointment needs, integrations, and implementation complexity.

## Small Business Division

**Best fit:** businesses with approximately 1-3 employees that need straightforward call routing, after-hours coverage, basic intake, or appointment support without complex CRM integration.

## When to escalate a deal

Escalate to Sales Management or Implementation before quoting when the prospect:

- Requires an integration with a specific CRM, EHR, POS, dispatch platform, or custom software.
- Operates in a regulated or high-risk industry.
- Needs multi-location routing, multilingual call handling, large volume, or custom reporting.
- Requests legal, financial, medical, or compliance guarantees.
- Requests a feature, timeline, pricing exception, or contract term not listed in the approved package materials.

## Positioning language

Use: “We can show you a tailored demo and confirm what is practical for your workflow.”

Avoid: “It can do anything,” “It is fully compliant,” “It will definitely replace staff,” “You will never miss a lead again,” or any guaranteed revenue/savings claim.
