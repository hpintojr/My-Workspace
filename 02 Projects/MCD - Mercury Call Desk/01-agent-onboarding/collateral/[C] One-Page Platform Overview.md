# Mercury Call Desk — One-Page Overview

**Never miss another opportunity on the phone.**

Mercury Call Desk is an AI-assisted telephony and call-desk platform that answers calls, routes customers, captures leads, and books appointments — 24/7.

## The problem we solve
- Calls go to voicemail after hours, at lunch, or when the team is slammed.
- Leads are lost when no one answers fast enough.
- Staff spend hours on repetitive front-desk questions.
- Call handling is inconsistent across people and locations.

## What it does
- **Answers & greets** callers with a human-like AI assistant, in your brand's voice.
- **Routes & transfers** to the right person or department.
- **Captures leads** and logs details automatically.
- **Books appointments** and handles common questions.
- **Summarizes** calls via SMS/email so nothing slips.
- **140+ languages** and CRM integrations available by plan.

## Who it's for
Home & field services, legal and professional services, medical/wellness practices, restaurants & hospitality, auto & dispatch, property management, and more — any business that runs on inbound calls and appointments.

## How it works
1. **Discovery & demo** — we learn your call flow and show a sample assistant tailored to a business like yours.
2. **Setup** — we configure your assistant, routing, and any integrations.
3. **Go live** — your AI call desk starts capturing calls and booking appointments, with summaries to your team.

## Why businesses choose us
- Coverage 24/7 without adding front-desk headcount immediately.
- Faster first response = fewer lost leads.
- Flexible plans from solo operators to multi-location and enterprise.
