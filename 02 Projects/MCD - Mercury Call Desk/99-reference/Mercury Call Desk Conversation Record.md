## 2. Outbound Sales Script Framework

The cold calling strategy relies on an objection-minimizing, curiosity-driven approach. By offering a risk-free entry point ("no-cost demo"), the script lowers barriers to entry and maximizes commitment rates.

### The "No-Cost Demo" Cold Call Script

> **Agent:** "Hi, I was just wondering what time you guys close."
>
> **Prospect:** "[Provides closing time / answers question]"
>
> **Agent:** "Got it. If a potential client calls you after hours, does someone pick up, or does the call roll to voicemail?
>
> You see, we help businesses like yours capture every lead and automate customer calls 24/7 with human-like AI assistants. This saves your team valuable time and ensures you never miss a client to the competition, meaning the platform essentially pays for itself.
>
> Would you be interested in a brief, **no-cost demo** to see how it could work for you?"

## 5. Legal Protection & Platform Security

To safeguard proprietary assets in an increasingly competitive market, the onboarding sequence mandates two core covenants before platform access is granted:

1. **Intellectual Property Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA):** Explicitly protects proprietary price charts, unique pitch scripts, tailored objection handlers, and outbound sales scripts. Sharing or reproducing these assets without explicit written consent is strictly prohibited.
2. **Vertical Non-Compete Clause:** A strict **3-month non-compete window** prevents departing agents from working with or establishing competing AI telephony operations targeting the same client base.
